Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 25, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to SunQuest's Privacy Policy. SunQuest is a software solution designed to help you determine direction when your device's magnetometer (compass) is not working properly. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your data is handled securely and transparently.

Information We Collect

SunQuest collects the following data to provide and improve our service:

  • Device orientation data (for basic direction sensing)
  • Time and date information (for accurate sun position calculations)
  • Device location (only when actively using the app)
  • Device information (model and brand)
  • Device settings (for optimal app performance)

How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used for:

  • Calculating the sun's position to determine direction
  • Providing alternative direction finding when magnetometer is unavailable
  • Determining your current orientation
  • Improving app performance and accuracy
  • Analyzing device compatibility and performance

Data Collection Preferences

You have control over your data collection preferences:

  • You will be asked about data collection when you first launch the app
  • You can change your preference at any time through the About box (menu → About)
  • Your choice is stored locally on your device
  • You can enable or disable data collection at any time

Data Storage and Security

SunQuest stores and processes data on our secure servers. We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information. We do not:

  • Share your personal information with third parties without your consent
  • Use your data for advertising purposes
  • Store your location history beyond what is necessary for the service

Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access your stored data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out of data collection at any time
  • Request information about how your data is being used

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact us:

  • By email: sunquest@codehospital.com
  • By visiting our website: sunquest.codehospital.com
